Our History

TechCon began in 2008 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world. Over the years, it has served as a platform for innovation, education, and networking.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to bring together tech professionals, enthusiasts, and companies to foster collaboration, innovation, and growth in the tech industry. Our goal is to inspire and educate the next generation of leaders through knowledge sharing and groundbreaking ideas.

Notable Past Speakers

Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google, shared his insights on artificial intelligence and its impact on global industries during his talk in 2019.

Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Facebook, discussed the role of women in tech leadership and the importance of inclusive work cultures in her 2020 address.
